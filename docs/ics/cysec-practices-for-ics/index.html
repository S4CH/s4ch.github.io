Learning Objectives#

As some of you may be aware, solid cybersecurity practices have a broad impact. It’s critical to include as many solid risk mitigation strategy as feasible into your daily routine. Cybersecurity methods are used in a variety of professions and situations, as well as in your personal life.

Focus on the few things like#

Describe cybersecurity practices and why they are important.

Describe common information-collection techniques and how to protect yourself from them.

Identify several methods to protect critical information.

List ways to physically protect critical assets at work, at home, and while traveling.

Identify specific ways to maintain integrity in secured areas.
